Temperature-induced errors in ITTC model-ship extrapolation

Journal of Marine Science and Engineering · IF 2.8 · Q2 · 2025
Sangseok Han, S. Dai, M. Terziev, et al.
University of Strathclyde · Inha University

Abstract

This study investigates temperature-dependent Reynolds number effects in towing-tank experiments and their implications for ITTC-based model–ship extrapolation procedures. Using high-fidelity CFD simulations, systematic resistance biases induced by temperature variations are quantified, highlighting limitations of conventional extrapolation practices.
